About

I bridge the gap between atoms and algorithms. As a final-year Materials Engineering student at IIT Jodhpur, I don't just study materials; I simulate, predict, and optimize them using cutting-edge AI.

From predicting the mechanical properties of graphene to monitoring additive manufacturing in real-time, I leverage data to accelerate discovery. My work spans computational materials science, machine learning, and materials informatics, aiming to solve complex challenges through data-driven and physics-informed approaches.
